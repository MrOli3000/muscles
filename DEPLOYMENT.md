# Deploy to GitHub Pages (For Phone Access)

## Quick Setup - Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to your repository settings: https://github.com/MrOli3000/muscles/settings/pages

2. Under "Build and deployment":
   - **Source**: Select "Deploy from a branch"
   - **Branch**: Select `main` and `/ (root)`
   - Click **Save**

3. Wait 1-2 minutes for deployment

4. Your app will be live at: **https://mrolli3000.github.io/muscles/**

## Using on Your Phone

Once deployed:
1. Open the URL on your phone browser (Safari, Chrome, etc.)
2. **Add to Home Screen** for quick access:
   - **iPhone**: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
   - **Android**: Tap Menu (⋮) → Add to Home Screen

3. It will work like a native app! Your data stays on your phone.

## Important Notes

- ✅ Works offline after first visit
- ✅ Data saved on your device (won't sync between devices)
- ✅ No login required
- ✅ Free forever (GitHub Pages is free for public repos)

## Alternative: Use a Different Hosting

If you prefer another service, you can also deploy to:
- **Netlify**: Drag and drop the files at https://app.netlify.com/drop
- **Vercel**: Connect your GitHub repo at https://vercel.com
- **Cloudflare Pages**: Similar process at https://pages.cloudflare.com

All of these will give you a URL you can access from your phone.

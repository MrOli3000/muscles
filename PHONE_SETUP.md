# 📱 Use Training Tracker on Your Phone

## Quick 3-Step Setup

### Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages (One-Time Setup)

Click this link to go directly to settings:
**https://github.com/MrOli3000/muscles/settings/pages**

Then:
1. Under "Source", select **GitHub Actions** (not "Deploy from a branch")
2. Click **Save**
3. Wait about 1 minute

### Step 2: Access Your App

Your training tracker will be live at:
**https://mrolli3000.github.io/muscles/**

Open this URL on your phone!

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen (Optional but Recommended)

For easy access like a real app:

**On iPhone:**
1. Open the URL in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Android:**
1. Open the URL in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

## ✅ You're Done!

Now you can:
- Open it from your home screen like any app
- Use it offline after the first visit
- Track your workouts at the gym
- All data stays on your phone (private and secure)

## Troubleshooting

**If the URL doesn't work:**
1. Check that you selected "GitHub Actions" as the source (not "Deploy from a branch")
2. Wait 2-3 minutes after saving
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Check the Actions tab: https://github.com/MrOli3000/muscles/actions

**If you see a 404 error:**
- The deployment might still be running
- Go to https://github.com/MrOli3000/muscles/actions and wait for the green checkmark
